## 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

Follow these practical tips to protect yourself from cybercrime and stay secure online:

### ✅ General Safety Tips
- 🔐 Use **strong and unique passwords** for every account (mix letters, numbers, symbols).
- 🔑 Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
- 📵 Avoid using **public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities** like banking or logging into personal accounts.
- 🧠 Think before you click – **don’t open suspicious links or attachments** from unknown sources.
- 📢 Do not share **personal information** (address, phone number, college details) publicly online.
- 🧾 Verify information before sharing to avoid **spreading fake news or misinformation**.
- 🧍 Be cautious of strangers online; **do not trust unknown profiles easily**.

### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- 💳 Never share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details** with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
- 📲 Always **verify payment requests** before approving them on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
- 🚫 Do not scan unknown **QR codes** or click on random payment links.
- 🏦 Use only **official banking and payment apps** downloaded from trusted app stores.

### 🚨 In Case of Cybercrime
- 🌐 Report immediately at: **https://cybercrime.gov.in**
- 📞 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**
- ⏱️ Act quickly to increase chances of recovering lost money or stopping misuse.

---

### ✔️ Reminder
Staying alert and informed is the best way to prevent cybercrime. Practice safe online habits every day!

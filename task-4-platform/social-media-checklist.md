Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use
✅ Do’s
Do think before you post and ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Do maintain a professional profile (use proper language and clear profile information).
Do protect your privacy by adjusting security settings.
Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Do engage positively and respectfully with others online.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly (phone number, address, passwords).
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Don’t spread fake news or unverified information.
Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
Don’t use social media excessively during study time or important tasks.

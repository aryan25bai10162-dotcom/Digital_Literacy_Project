# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do's

- ✔️ Think before you post – ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- ✔️ Protect your privacy – adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing sensitive information.
- ✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- ✔️ Be respectful in comments and discussions, even during disagreements.
- ✔️ Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and goals.

## ❌ Don'ts

- ❌ Do not share personal details like phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.
- ❌ Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or abusive content.
- ❌ Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
- ❌ Avoid oversharing your daily life or location publicly.
- ❌ Do not believe or spread unverified news or rumors.
